Robert Tangora's new book, The Internal Structure of Cloud Hands, has just been published.

Last October, I spoke to Robert about why he chose this topic for a Tai Chi book and what he hoped to communicate to people.

Based on that interview, working with him live doing his Cloud Hands training, and the meticulous way he approaches learning, teaching, and Tai Chi, I have no doubt that this book is going to the top of my recommended reading list next to Energy Gates.

Robert is offering up a free sample chapter from the book as a preview.

Once you've downloaded the chapter, he'll send you a link to the book on Amazon and while you're waiting for it in the mail, do what I did: re-watch the whole interview I did with him.

Inner Form members: this is going to be a juicy topic for us to discuss more. While you're waiting for the Tai Chi Mastery Program, the material in this book could easily fill all your free hours with rich, rewarding practice.
