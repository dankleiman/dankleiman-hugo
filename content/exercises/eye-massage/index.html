If you do a lot of visually demanding work, chances are you hold a lot of extra tension in your eyes. This exercise can be a nice way to gauge your tension levels, while simultaneously letting go and relaxing.

As will all the Disrupt Techniques in this series, sprinkle this exercise in throughout your day whenever you remember. It will stem the build-up of the stress snowball. Later on, these can be great warm-ups for more sophisticated practices.
