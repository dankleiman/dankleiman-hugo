How You Can Join


The really cool part is that the group meets each week to practice together. I'm sure you know the "workshop syndrome" of getting excited about something new you learn in a weekend, only to lose it a few months later because you never practice. It's hard to keep it up, but because these guys get together on a regular basis and get frequent input from instructors, they've done a great job developing their tai chi forms!

If you're interested in learning tai chi, not only will you get the depth of a workshop, you'll get the follow-through and support of a regular training group.
